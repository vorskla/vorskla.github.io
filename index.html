I was named Olena, born in the Soviet Union right as it was falling appart, with a mid-line defect. Ukrainian with a cleft palet and a list of other issues but the familly decided not to give the baby up (though the option was kindly mentioned by the nurses). This child continued growing, life-loving, friendly and kind until about the age of three. Being a Ukrainian with a facial deformity apperently was a crime punishable by mokery, bullying and abuse. But all of that is in the past. Stan Lee and Stephen Hawking are my heroes. I am very passionate about causes, such as people (to include women) in stem. I'm sure my time in the Army going to Iraq, Afghanistant and serving on the mexican border ission helped me improve my character and grow as a person and a human. And on with the story... Yaroslava Teslia was my first friend. I was lucky to gain more wonderful friends as the time went on - Anna, Liuba, Marina, Karina really made a big difference as if it was not for them I would have never went outside other tha to drag myself to school.

Really getting back to Story telling (just like Stan Lee one day, right? lolz) and coding now, as there is nothing stopping me anymore, if ever (lol).
